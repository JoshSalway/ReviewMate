Connect your Google listing

We need your Google Place ID so we can pull in your reviews and generate your review request link. This takes 2 minutes.

💡 How to find your Google Place ID
▼
1
Go to Google Maps and search for your business name
2
Click on your business listing to open the info panel
3
Look at the URL in your browser — it will contain a section that starts with 1s0x — that's your Place ID
4
Alternatively, go to developers.google.com/maps/documentation/javascript/examples/places-placeid-finder and search your business there
Starts with "ChIJ" — copy it exactly from Google Maps
