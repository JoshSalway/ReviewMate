Connect your Google listing

Your Google Place ID lets us pull in your reviews and create your direct review link. Takes 2 minutes to find.

💡 How to find your Google Place ID
▼
1
Go to Google Maps and search for your business name
2
Click on your business listing in the results
3
Look at the URL — find the part after /place/ — copy that whole section
4
Or use the official Place ID finder: developers.google.com/maps/documentation/javascript/examples/places-placeid-finder
5
Your Place ID will start with "ChIJ" — paste it below
